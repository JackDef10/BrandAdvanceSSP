Diversity Online Video

We can run video campaigns across Pre-roll, Inslide and Incontent formats. By combining all 3 formats we ensure our clients are reaching as many people as possible from our various diverse communities.

Instream Video

Instream Video (pre-roll) - While vulnerable to ad blockers, instream is still the most reliable and frequently used system for placing video ads within a relevant video context.

Outstream Video

Outstream Video (Incontent & Inslide) - Outstream video ads appear in non-video environments like social feeds, text-based editorial content, and similar. Outstream has a huge advantage as it can deliver video ads anywhere and everywhere.



Diversity Live Stream Video

➤ During troubled times online/live streaming videos are becoming a more requested solution.
➤ Reach your clients in new ways, from the safety or their homes.
➤ Promote live streams in additional places (besides youtube and twitch) in order to target new audiences.
➤ Combine a live stream with live data (available products, offers and betting numbers for example) in one collected solution.
➤ Display the LIVE stream with or without an additional ad/overlay. It can be text, images, data feeds etc.

Partnerships (Teads / Sublime / Connected DSP's)

Teads

By connecting Brand Advance's network of diverse publishers with Teads' global demand from some of the world’s top advertisers, the partnership aims to drive revenue, support publishers' growth through premium monetization, and connect brands to more diverse audiences whilst maintaining the high standards of a brand safe, quality environment that customers expect. Teads’ end to end technology provides a curated ecosystem by connecting quality publishers and brands. Ad Quality & Brand Safety

The BA Media Network has been built to facilitate bringing diversity media into a Brand Safe environment - selected diversity publications that pass our ‘On-Boarding Standards’

OUR SYSTEMS PROPRIETARY MACHINE LEARNING (AD FRAUD)

Identify anonymous IP addresses via frequently updated database provided by a third party, incl. reported proxies check for multiple impressions from same party (using a number of factors) IP mismatch, impression IP different from click IP user with multiple IP addresses (based on a number of factors) IP with high click through rate spoofed browsers - detected capabilities vary from expected user with multiple user agents assessment of common user agents continual refinement of algorithms and strategies

Technology

VIEWABILITY - The BA Network includes industry standard viewability reporting - Reporting whether or not ads were seen by legitimate people, verified to a high standard. URL TARGETING - our platform allows URL targeting to control the domains and specific ad’s that appear on them. MOAT TAGS - Our platform allows these tags to run alongside campaigns.

Relationship

We maintain a high quality direct publisher->agency relationship ensuring that we understand who are publishers are and who their audiences are - we constantly work with all our network updating and improving their digital offering and ensuring above industry standards are maintained.

BA has adopted our own internal tagging of articles - XYZ Tagging is a system we use across our network to ensure each article posted across our diversity portfolio is within a Green , Amber, Red system.

  1. Green

    Nothing of concern

  2. Amber

    Topless content / Artistic or editorial / Mild Viewpoints on political point.

  3. Red

    Controversial point of view / Very Artistic nudity - *All publications and Content have no past or present offensive language / views or opinion / or violent content)

The BA platform allows IP blocking however due to our network being a PMP - our onboarding process mitigates against this being required.

We also allow the use of keywords - so we can stop ads from displaying within articles our clients request.
